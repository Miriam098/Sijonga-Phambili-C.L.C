Welcome to Sijonga Phambili

Sijonga-Phambili Community Learning Centre is an official institution of the Western Cape Education Department. It is a night school based in Hout bay and has been passionately serving the community since 1995, the centre has helped and educated thousands of people within and out of the community to obtain their Grade 12 aswell as their ABET L3 & L4 (Grade 9)The aim of this centre is to give everyone a second chance to complete their schooling and enable them to go into further studies. Our goal is educate as many people in and out of Hout Bay as possible.
